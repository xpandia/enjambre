# ENJAMBRE — VIDEO STORYBOARD
## 60-90 Second Sizzle Reel
### The Vertex Swarm Challenge 2026

**Tone:** Cinematic. Urgent. Human. Think Apple product film meets National Geographic.
**Music:** Builds from a single acoustic guitar to a full electronic/orchestral crescendo. Latin percussion undertone throughout.
**Color grade:** Warm earth tones for farm scenes, cool blue-white for tech scenes. High contrast.
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (with 1:1 and 9:16 cuts for social)

---

## ACT I — THE PROBLEM (0:00 — 0:25)

### SHOT 1 (0:00 — 0:05)
**Aerial drone shot. Golden hour. Endless farmland stretching to the horizon.**

*SUPER: "Latin America. 130 million hectares of farmland."*

Sound: Wind. Birds. Silence.

---

### SHOT 2 (0:05 — 0:10)
**Ground level. A farmer walks between rows of crops. Worn boots. Calloused hands touching a diseased leaf. He holds it up — brown spots, curling edges.**

*No text. Let the image speak.*

Sound: Footsteps on dry soil.

---

### SHOT 3 (0:10 — 0:15)
**Wide shot. Same farmer, now a tiny figure in a massive field. The scale is overwhelming. One person. Thousands of hectares.**

*SUPER: "$65 billion lost every year."*

Sound: Music begins — low, sparse, a single note.

---

### SHOT 4 (0:15 — 0:20)
**Quick cuts (1-2 seconds each):**
- Dead crops in a dried furrow
- A truck driving slowly along an unpaved road between fields
- Hands writing in a paper notebook
- Pesticide being sprayed broadly, wastefully, from a backpack sprayer

*SUPER: "Outdated tools. Wasted resources. Blind decisions."*

Sound: Music builds slightly. A clock ticking faintly underneath.

---

### SHOT 5 (0:20 — 0:25)
**Black screen. 2 seconds.**

*SUPER (white text, centered): "What if the field could see itself?"*

Sound: Silence. Then a single electronic pulse.

---

## ACT II — THE SOLUTION (0:25 — 0:55)

### SHOT 6 (0:25 — 0:30)
**A drone lifts off from a dirt clearing at the edge of a farm. Close-up on propellers spinning up. Dust kicks up.**

Then a second drone. Then a third. Then ten more. They rise together.

Sound: The swarm hum. Propellers layered into something almost musical. The soundtrack drops to let the sound dominate.

---

### SHOT 7 (0:30 — 0:37)
**Aerial shot looking DOWN on the swarm as it spreads across the field. The drones fan out in a coordinated pattern — like cells dividing, like a flock of starlings.**

*SUPER: "Enjambre. Decentralized drone swarms."*

Overlay animation: Thin lines connect the drones, showing the mesh network forming in real-time. Voronoi partitions appear translucently over the field.

Sound: Music swells. Electronic beat kicks in — precise, rhythmic, confident.

---

### SHOT 8 (0:37 — 0:42)
**Close-up: A drone's camera feed. We see crops from above — green rows, then a patch of discoloration. A bounding box appears around the anomaly. Text overlay on the feed: "Anthracnose detected. Confidence: 94%."**

Cut to: A second drone arriving from another angle, confirming the detection.

Sound: A subtle "lock-on" tone. Satisfying. Precise.

---

### SHOT 9 (0:42 — 0:47)
**Screen recording of the FleetCloud dashboard. Clean UI. The map shows:**
- Drones as moving dots
- Scanned areas turning from gray to green
- The anomaly zone highlighted in amber
- A report card populating: disease name, location, recommended action

*SUPER: "AI that sees what humans miss."*

Sound: Keys typing. Data populating. A sense of speed and clarity.

---

### SHOT 10 (0:47 — 0:52)
**The farmer from Shot 2. He is now standing still, looking at his phone. The screen shows the Enjambre alert — in Spanish, with a clean map and clear instructions.**

He looks up from the phone toward the field. A slight nod. The beginning of a smile.

*No text. The emotion is the message.*

Sound: Music softens momentarily. Human beat.

---

### SHOT 11 (0:52 — 0:55)
**Split screen or rapid intercut:**

| Left | Right |
|---|---|
| Old way: farmer walking for hours | New way: swarm covering the field in minutes |
| Old way: broad pesticide spray | New way: targeted, precise intervention |
| Old way: paper notebook | New way: real-time dashboard |

*SUPER: "3 days becomes 47 minutes."*

Sound: Beat drops. Percussive. Confident.

---

## ACT III — THE VISION (0:55 — 1:15/1:30)

### SHOT 12 (0:55 — 1:02)
**Epic aerial shot. Sunrise. A massive swarm — 50+ drones — launches across a sprawling farm. The camera pulls back to reveal the scale: the swarm is a living organism moving across the landscape.**

*SUPER: "From 12 drones to 12,000. From one farm to a continent."*

Sound: Full orchestral + electronic crescendo. This is the goosebumps moment.

---

### SHOT 13 (1:02 — 1:08)
**Quick montage — real places, real faces:**
- A cooperative meeting in Mexico, farmers gathered around a laptop
- A drone tech calibrating equipment in a Brazilian field at dawn
- A data analyst in Bogota reviewing crop health maps
- Hands shaking — a partnership sealed

*SUPER: "Mexico. Brazil. Colombia. Argentina."*

Sound: Music sustains its peak. Latin percussion comes forward.

---

### SHOT 14 (1:08 — 1:15)
**Return to the farmer from Shot 2. He is walking his field again — but this time, he is calm. Above him, the swarm is already working. He looks up.**

**Camera tilts up from his face to the sky, where drones move in perfect formation against an orange sunset.**

Sound: Music resolves. Clean. Hopeful.

---

### SHOT 15 (1:15 — 1:25) [CLOSING CARD]

**Black screen.**

The Enjambre logo assembles — built from small hexagonal cells that connect and lock together (like a honeycomb, like a swarm).

*SUPER:*

> **ENJAMBRE**
> Coordina. Cultiva. Conquista.

*Below: enjambre.ag*

Sound: Final note. Resonant. Lingers for 2 seconds into silence.

---

### SHOT 16 (1:25 — 1:30) [OPTIONAL TAG]

**Black screen. Small white text.**

*"Latin American farms feed 600 million people. It is time to give them the tools they deserve."*

Sound: Silence.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Total runtime** | 75 seconds (sweet spot), expandable to 90 |
| **Resolution** | 4K master, 1080p delivery |
| **Drone footage** | DJI Mavic 3 Pro or Air 3 for cinematic aerials |
| **Screen recordings** | 60fps, clean UI, no cursor hesitation |
| **VO option** | No voiceover in primary cut. Text supers carry the narrative. VO version as alternate for presentations. |
| **Subtitles** | Spanish and English hardcoded versions |
| **Deliverables** | 16:9 (main), 9:16 (Instagram/TikTok), 1:1 (LinkedIn), 30s cut (ads) |
| **Music licensing** | Artlist or Epidemic Sound. Track must feel both technological and rooted in the land. |
